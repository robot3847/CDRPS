ANIME OF THE WEEK*

Inuyashiki

Ichiro Inuyashiki is a 58-year-old man, with a fairly routine life, an office worker and a family that practically despises him. One day, after a medical checkup, he discovers that he has terminal cancer and that he has little time to live. As if that were not enough, on the way home... An alien ship lands on top of him! Upon awakening, Inuyashiki is no longer the same: he has been rebuilt as a killing machine. But with a new body comes great responsibility: will Inuyashiki become a hero or perhaps humanity's worst nightmare?
